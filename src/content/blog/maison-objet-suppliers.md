In September 2018, I went for the first time to Maison & Objet, an international interior trade show in Paris for the trade. This was the opportunity for me to scour the market and find new suppliers.

You may have seen many pictures from it on my Instagram, but I thought I would share with you the suppliers I liked. So in no particular order, here are four brands I particularly liked.

Trade shows like Maison & Objet are invaluable for discovering craftsmanship, new materials, and suppliers who can bring a project from concept to reality. For clients, that means access to pieces you will not find on the high street — and interiors with real individuality.

I will continue to share sourcing notes and inspiration from shows, showrooms, and site visits as the blog develops.
